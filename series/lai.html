Learning in the Age of AI

AI is already in Vietnamese classrooms. This series doesn't ask whether to use it — it asks what happens to learning when we do. Every episode is anchored to a learning theory, not a tool list.

About this series

LAI is built on a conviction: AI in education deserves serious thinking, not just enthusiasm or panic. Each episode takes one learning theory and asks what it reveals about AI-assisted learning.
